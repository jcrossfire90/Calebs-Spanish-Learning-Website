Lesson 5: Days of the Week

Learn the days of the week and how to talk about schedules

Why Learn the Days of the Week?

The days of the week are used every day in Spanish. You will hear them when talking about:

  • Appointments
  • Work schedules
  • School schedules
  • Birthdays
  • Future plans

Days of the Week

Spanish English
Lunes Monday
Martes Tuesday
Miércoles Wednesday
Jueves Thursday
Viernes Friday
Sábado Saturday
Domingo Sunday

💡 Spanish Note

Unlike English, the days of the week are not normally capitalized in Spanish.

Examples:

  • lunes
  • martes
  • miércoles

For learning purposes, the days may sometimes appear capitalized in this course to make them easier to identify.
