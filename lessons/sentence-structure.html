Learn how basic Spanish sentences are formed

Basic Spanish Sentence Pattern

Most simple Spanish sentences follow this pattern:

Subject + Verb + Extra Information

Spanish English
Yo estudio español. I study Spanish.
Ella habla inglés. She speaks English.
Nosotros vivimos en Texas. We live in Texas.
